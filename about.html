ABOUT US

Represented by two playful hedgehogs symbolizing the Chinese Yin and Yang, SONIC unites the ideals of balance, community, and fun within the diverse growing world of digital currency. As the world embraces the play-to-earn narrative, SONIC empowers users to directly participate, compete, and thrive together in a competitive and rewarding environment
